Dedicated to Women's Health for Over 15 Years

Dr. Sheetal Talekar is a seasoned Consultant Gynecologist and Obstetrician based in Vapi, Gujarat. With extensive academic training and clinical experience, she has dedicated her career to providing comprehensive, compassionate women's healthcare — from routine gynecological checkups to complex high-risk pregnancies and advanced laparoscopic procedures.

Her patient-first philosophy, combined with evidence-based medicine, has made her one of the most trusted gynecologists in the Vapi-Silvassa region. She believes in educating her patients at every step, ensuring they are informed and empowered participants in their own healthcare journey.

Our Medical Services

A full spectrum of gynecological and obstetric services under one roof, delivered with expertise and care.

Antenatal & Prenatal Care

Complete pregnancy management from first trimester to delivery — regular monitoring, ultrasounds, and nutritional guidance for a healthy pregnancy.

PregnancyUltrasoundNutrition

Normal & Cesarean Delivery

Safe, monitored deliveries with a focus on mother and baby wellbeing. Both vaginal and C-section deliveries performed with precision and care.

Normal DeliveryC-SectionNeonatal Care

High-Risk Pregnancy

Specialized care for pregnancies complicated by diabetes, hypertension, multiple gestation, or fetal anomalies. Close monitoring for safe outcomes.

Gestational DiabetesPreeclampsiaTwins

Laparoscopic Surgery

Minimally invasive surgical treatment for fibroids, ovarian cysts, endometriosis, and ectopic pregnancy — faster recovery, minimal scars.

EndometriosisFibroidsOvarian Cysts

PCOS & Infertility Treatment

Hormonal evaluation, lifestyle intervention, and medical management for PCOS. Ovulation induction and fertility workup services available.

PCOSInfertilityOvulation

Cervical Cancer Screening

Pap smear, colposcopy, and HPV screening for early detection and prevention of cervical cancer and other gynecological malignancies.

Pap SmearHPVColposcopy

Menstrual Disorders

Diagnosis and treatment of irregular periods, heavy bleeding, painful periods (dysmenorrhea), and premenstrual syndrome (PMS).
